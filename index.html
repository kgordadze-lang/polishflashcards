Privacy Notice

Po polsku is designed to help you learn Polish, not to collect your personal information.

  • No Data Collection: We do not ask for your email, we do not track your behavior, and we do not use marketing or analytics cookies.
  • Local Storage Only: Any progress you make (like your flashcard scores) is saved locally on your own device. It is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Standard Server Logs: Like almost all websites, our hosting provider temporarily logs basic technical requests (such as IP addresses) strictly to ensure the website functions securely and reliably. We do not access or use this information to identify anyone.
About

I'm a foreigner living in Poland, and somewhere between advancing a master's degree and trying to navigate seven consecutive Polish consonants, I decided to put my neuroplasticity to the test and learn the language properly.

Flashcards were the thing that finally made vocabulary stick for me, so I built a small, mobile-first app around exactly that - every word paired with a sentence from real daily life (heavily inspired by situations where I gracefully panicked), plus built-in audio so you can hear the pronunciation.

It's completely free, needs no account, and collects zero data. I'll keep expanding the materials as I go.

Install
Install in one tap
Your browser can add Po polsku for you - no steps needed.

Po polsku installs straight from your browser - one tap to open, full screen, and it works offline. No app store, no account.

iPhone & iPad (Safari)
  1. Open popolsku.app in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share icon - the square with an upward arrow.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add in the top-right corner.
Android (Chrome)
  1. Open popolsku.app in Chrome.
  2. Tap the menu - three dots in the top-right.
  3. Tap Add to Home screen (or Install app).
  4. Tap Add or Install to confirm.

Once it's added, open Po polsku from your home screen like any other app - it launches full screen and runs offline.
